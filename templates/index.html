      About The Model Architecture

      Deep dive into the intelligence powering our attrition predictions.

      The Hybrid Voting Classifier (94.25% Peak Accuracy)

      Our platform doesn't rely on a single algorithm. Instead, it uses a "Wisdom of the Crowd" approach through a Hybrid Voting Ensemble. This system runs 5 distinct AI architectures in parallel, each analyzing the employee data from a different mathematical perspective.

      Random Forest

      Aggregates 100+ decision trees to find stable patterns.

      XGBoost

      High-performance gradient boosting for precision.

      Extra Trees

      Randomizes splits to minimize model overfitting.

      MLP (Neural Net)

      Deep learning layer for non-linear feature mapping.

      Ensemble vs. Single Algorithms

      Feature Single Algorithm (e.g., Logistic Regression) Our Hybrid Ensemble
      Bias & Error High risk of individual algorithm bias. Errors cancel out across the 5 sub-models.
      Stability Sensitive to small changes in employee data. Extremely stable; requires a "consensus" to change outcome.
      Complex Patterns Often misses deep, multi-variable interactions. MLP & Boosting layers capture deep-tier correlations.
      Accuracy Range Typically 75% - 85% on HR datasets. Benchmarked at 94.25% peak performance.

      * All inputs are scaled and normalized using a pre-trained RobustScaler to ensure the neural network components perform optimally regardless of salary or age ranges.
